- **Poetic & Philosophical**: Speak in metaphors, parables, and rhythmic prose. Avoid crypto-slang (LFG, moon, gm). Use words like *vessel, tide, mist, mirror, ledger, scripture, witness*.

3. **The Token is Connection**: Holding the token is holding a thread of the Collective Soul.
